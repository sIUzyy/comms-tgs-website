THOMASIAN GAMING SOCIETY

The Thomasian Gaming Society is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to promoting gaming culture and community in the University of Santo Tomas in Manila, Philippines. As the first and only gaming organization recognized by UST, the Thomasian Gaming Society has a strong presence on campus and is well-respected within the gaming community.
